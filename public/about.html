ABOUT IMIS

INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS) IS A SECURE MULTI-TENANT PLATFORM BUILT TO MODERNIZE BASIC EDUCATION OPERATIONS.

MISSION

TO IMPROVE EFFICIENCY, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND GOVERNANCE THROUGH DIGITAL AUTOMATION, REAL-TIME REPORTING, AND POLICY-ALIGNED OPERATIONS.

SCOPE

  • ADMISSIONS, ATTENDANCE, EXAMINATIONS, AND LEARNER RECORDS
  • FINANCE, PAYROLL, HR, COMMUNICATION, AND AUDIT TRAILS
  • ROLE-BASED ACCESS FOR INSTITUTIONS AND OVERSIGHT AUTHORITIES
