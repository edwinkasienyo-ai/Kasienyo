IMIS PRIVACY POLICY

THIS POLICY EXPLAINS HOW IMIS COLLECTS, USES, STORES, AND PROTECTS INFORMATION FOR BASIC EDUCATION MANAGEMENT.

1. DATA WE PROCESS

  • LEARNER, STAFF, AND INSTITUTIONAL ADMINISTRATIVE DATA
  • AUTHENTICATION DATA (USERNAME, SECURITY EVENTS, OTP LOGS)
  • ACADEMIC, ATTENDANCE, AND FINANCIAL OPERATIONAL RECORDS

2. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

DATA IS USED TO DELIVER AUTHORIZED EDUCATION ADMINISTRATION, COMPLIANCE REPORTING, INSTITUTION OPERATIONS, AND AUDITABLE GOVERNANCE.

3. SECURITY CONTROLS

IMIS APPLIES ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL, AUTHENTICATION WITH OTP, AUDIT LOGGING, AND ACCESS SCOPING TO MINIMIZE UNAUTHORIZED EXPOSURE.

4. DATA RETENTION

RETENTION IS DRIVEN BY REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS, OPERATIONAL NEEDS, AND INSTITUTIONAL POLICY.
