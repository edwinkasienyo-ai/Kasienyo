INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS) FOR BASIC EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS
THE INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS) IS A SECURE, INTELLIGENT, AND FULLY INTEGRATED DIGITAL PLATFORM DESIGNED TO ENHANCE EFFICIENCY, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND GOVERNANCE WITHIN BASIC EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS. IT PROVIDES A CENTRALIZED, AUTOMATED ENVIRONMENT THAT STREAMLINES OPERATIONS WHILE PROMOTING ACCURACY, TRANSPARENCY, AND REAL-TIME ACCESS TO CRITICAL INFORMATION FOR EFFECTIVE DECISION-MAKING.
DEVELOPED IN STRICT COMPLIANCE WITH THE BASIC EDUCATION REGULATIONS, 2015 (LEGAL NOTICE NO. 39 OF 2015), NOTABLY CLAUSES 61, 62, AND 63, THE SYSTEM ENSURES PROPER MAINTENANCE OF LEARNER BIO-DATA, PARENT/GUARDIAN RECORDS, AND INSTITUTIONAL REGISTERS IN HARD AND ELECTRONIC FORMATS, SAFEGUARDING DATA INTEGRITY AND SUPPORTING A SEAMLESS SHIFT FROM MANUAL TO DIGITAL RECORD MANAGEMENT.
ALIGNED WITH KENYA'S CBC/CBE, IMIS INTEGRATES ADMISSIONS, ATTENDANCE, ASSESSMENTS, EXAMINATIONS, FINANCE, PAYROLL, BEHAVIORAL MONITORING, AND ANALYTICS. THROUGH ROLE-BASED ACCESS, IT SECURELY CONNECTS ADMINISTRATORS, TEACHERS, LEARNERS, PARENTS, BOM, SUPPORT STAFF, AND SERVICE PROVIDERS WHILE ALIGNING OPERATIONS WITH MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TSC OVERSIGHT FOR COMPLIANCE, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE.
