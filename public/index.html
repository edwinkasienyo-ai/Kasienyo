INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM
FOR THE BASIC LEARNING INSTITUTIONS
IMIS is a smart, secure, digital platform for modern education management
The Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) is a secure, intelligent, and fully integrated digital platform designed to enhance operational efficiency, strengthen accountability, and promote effective governance within basic education learning institutions. It offers a centralized and automated environment that streamlines institutional processes, improves data accuracy, and ensures transparency across all levels of management.
By enabling real-time access to critical information, the system empowers administrators, educators, and stakeholders to make timely, informed decisions. It simplifies record management, supports effective planning, and enhances overall institutional performance through reliable and easily accessible data.
The system is developed in strict compliance with the Basic Education Regulations, 2015 (Legal Notice No. 39 of 2015), particularly clauses 61, 62, and 63, which mandate that all basic learning institutions maintain both physical and electronic records. In alignment with these legal requirements, the IMIS ensures proper documentation, secure storage, and efficient retrieval of institutional records, thereby supporting compliance, continuity, and integrity in educational management.
