INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS)

FOR BASIC LEARNING INSTITUTIONS

A smart, secure, scalable digital platform for modern education management.
IMIS unifies administration, academics, finance, and communication for basic learning institutions with role-based access for administrators, teachers, learners, parents, BoM, and support staff.
Admissions, attendance, assessments, fees, payroll, analytics, CBC-aligned reporting, SMS, and M-Pesa-ready workflows built for reliable, secure operations in public and private schools.
Governance: Teachers Service Commission (TSC) and Ministry of Education officials retain policy and compliance oversight; each Head of Institution (HoI) manages institution-level operations with accountable school-level dashboards.
