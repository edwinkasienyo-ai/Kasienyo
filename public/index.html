INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS)
FOR BASIC EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS
THE INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (IMIS) IS A SECURE, INTELLIGENT, AND FULLY INTEGRATED DIGITAL PLATFORM DESIGNED TO ENHANCE EFFICIENCY, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND GOVERNANCE WITHIN BASIC EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS. IT PROVIDES A CENTRALIZED, AUTOMATED ENVIRONMENT THAT STREAMLINES INSTITUTIONAL OPERATIONS WHILE PROMOTING ACCURACY, TRANSPARENCY, AND REAL-TIME ACCESS TO CRITICAL INFORMATION FOR EFFECTIVE DECISION-MAKING.
DEVELOPED IN STRICT COMPLIANCE WITH THE BASIC EDUCATION REGULATIONS, 2015 (LEGAL NOTICE NO. 39 OF 2015), NOTABLY CLAUSES 61, 62, AND 63, THE SYSTEM ENSURES THE PROPER MAINTENANCE OF LEARNER BIO-DATA, PARENT AND GUARDIAN RECORDS, AND INSTITUTIONAL REGISTERS IN BOTH HARD AND ELECTRONIC FORMATS. IMIS OPERATIONALIZES THESE STATUTORY REQUIREMENTS BY SAFEGUARDING DATA INTEGRITY, ENSURING REGULATORY COMPLIANCE, AND FACILITATING A SEAMLESS TRANSITION FROM MANUAL TO DIGITIZED RECORD MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS.
THE PLATFORM IS FULLY ALIGNED WITH KENYA'S COMPETENCY-BASED CURRICULUM (CBC/CBE) AND INTEGRATES ALL CORE INSTITUTIONAL FUNCTIONS INTO A UNIFIED SYSTEM, INCLUDING ADMISSIONS, ATTENDANCE, ASSESSMENTS, EXAMINATIONS, FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT, PAYROLL PROCESSING, BEHAVIORAL MONITORING, AND ADVANCED ANALYTICS. THIS INTEGRATION FOSTERS EFFICIENCY, ENHANCES INSTITUTIONAL PERFORMANCE, AND SUPPORTS EVIDENCE-BASED PLANNING AND REPORTING.
THROUGH A ROBUST ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL FRAMEWORK, IMIS SECURELY CONNECTS ALL KEY STAKEHOLDERS, ADMINISTRATORS, TEACHERS, LEARNERS, PARENTS, BOARD OF MANAGEMENT (BOM) MEMBERS, SUPPORT STAFF, AND SERVICE PROVIDERS, WITHIN A SINGLE ECOSYSTEM. IT FURTHER ALIGNS INSTITUTIONAL OPERATIONS WITH NATIONAL REGULATORY FRAMEWORKS AND OVERSIGHT BODIES SUCH AS THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND THE TEACHERS SERVICE COMMISSION (TSC), THEREBY PROMOTING COMPLIANCE, ACCOUNTABILITY, AND OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE.
